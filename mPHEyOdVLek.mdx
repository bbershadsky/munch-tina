# Pickled Fish Fillet with Chinese Cabbage Side Dish

## Ingredients:

- Fish fillets (black fish, grass carp, or longli; tilapia as alternative)
- Salt
- White pepper
- Egg white
- Water starch
- Chinese cabbage
- Sauerkraut
- Onions
- Ginger
- Millet
- Chili
- Chicken stock
- Cooking wine
- Green onion

## Instructions:

1. Prepare the fish fillets by adding an appropriate amount of salt, white pepper, egg white, and water starch. Mix well.
2. Add some Chinese cabbage as a side dish. Pat it a few times to make it easier to taste.
3. Use sauerkraut from Costco (optional). Sauté onions, ginger, millet, and chili until fragrant.
4. Add the sauerkraut without the soup. Stir-fry dry and then add the cabbage. Stir-fry the cabbage until slightly broken.
5. Pour in the sour soup from the sauerkraut (excluding the soup) and chicken stock, about twice the amount of vegetables. Adjust salt, pepper, and white vinegar to taste.
6. Cook until desired flavor is achieved. If you don't like a strong sour taste, omit the white vinegar or cook for a few more minutes after boiling the soup.
7. Place all ingredients into a large bowl. Cook the fish fillets in batches if needed.
8. Pour cooking wine over the fish fillets when they turn completely white. Remove immediately to avoid breaking the fish fillets.
9. Arrange the cooked fish fillets on top of the side dishes and pour the fish fillets cooked for the second time into a large bowl with the soup.
10. Sprinkle green onion and serve.

Enjoy your Pickled Fish Fillet with Chinese Cabbage Side Dish!
